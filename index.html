About Us

The Spice Is Right Fencing & Retaining Walls is a locally owned business based in Victor Harbor, proudly servicing the Fleurieu Peninsula and surrounding areas.

We specialize in the construction of concrete sleeper and block retaining walls, as well as high-quality residential fencing.

Whenever possible, we use Australian-made products. We proudly work with Lysaght Steel for our fencing materials and Outback Sleepers in Lonsdale, SA for our retaining walls.

We're passionate about supporting local industry—choosing Australian-made helps ensure premium quality while keeping Australians in work.
